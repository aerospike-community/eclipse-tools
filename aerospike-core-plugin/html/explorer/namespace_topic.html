Namespaces

Records and sets are grouped into namespaces -- super collections of records.

Namespaces are policy containers, that is, the namespace configuration dictates how and where records are stored (DRAM or Flash memory), along with some policies (for example, the replication factor) that dictate the behavior of the data in the namespace. Depending on your application(s), you can have multiple namespaces.
